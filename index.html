Hyperspace Dance

My name is Annie Wang and Hyperspace Dance is my choreography project. My goal is to create work that transforms the space that it inhabits. I am inspired by storytelling - the constant progression from one state to another. Transition, process, struggle, reaction, the moment of decision, the act of remaining, the stillness in a moment that is about to change. All these are the components of stories, whether they come from the human world, from nature, from mathematics, or from dreams. I believe that at its best, storytelling through the physicality of dance creates an intimate, charged space that goes beyond its own boundaries and enfolds the audience.
